üìú THE STORY OF MOMO

MOMO - MAIN CHARACTER

Momo is the main character who struggles with negative emotions often leading to toxic positivity. Neglectful parents and an unstable family situation instilled in him a feeling of worthlessness, which he still struggles with.

He tries to find meaning in his life through new people, but his inability to form genuine connections due to his insincerity creates more problems than fulfillment. The middleschooler is faced with feelings of melancholy and questions about the meaning of life.

The story begins and centers around the high school where the main character, Momo, studies, as well as around ghostly places from the depths of memories of the past. Momo wants to be liked by everyone, but it's not that easy when you can't accept yourself.
